How It Works

Network Next Gen is a referral only group of 20's-30's in the design industry. We host 4 dinners a year for about 15-20 designers, one per firm. We go around the table and each discuss our backgrounds and how we got to where we are, and then we go through various design topics. We always leave inspired and with amazing new relationships.

At the end of the year, we have a party where we bring together all past Network Next Gen attendees to reconnect and meet other Network Next Gen'ers.

Despite our invite only policy, we love meeting new people. If you are interested in attending one of our dinners, please reach out to us here.
